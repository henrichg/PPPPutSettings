**Android application - PPPPutSettings for PhoneProfilesPlus**

Helper application for configure Android settings parameters.
With this application is not needed rooted device.

*** IMPORTANT ***

- It is "plugin", so does NOT work standalone!
- Designed for co-working with 'PhoneProfilesPlus' application. Without it, this application do nothing.
